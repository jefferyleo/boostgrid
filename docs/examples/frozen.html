Frozen left columns

Pin one or more leading columns to the viewport edge while the rest scroll horizontally. Frozen columns must come first in column order; Boostgrid computes their cumulative left offsets at render time and applies position: sticky. A drop-shadow appears on the rightmost frozen cell only after the user scrolls.
